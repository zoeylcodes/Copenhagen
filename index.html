Why Copenhagen?

Copenhagen, the City of Hygge

Finding hygge in Copenhagen is about embracing the Danish way of life, where warmth and simplicity are key. As you wander the city's cobblestone streets, you'll find cozy cafés filled with soft lighting and warm blankets, perfect for enjoying a cup of coffee and a pastry. The atmosphere of hygge is woven into the everyday life of Copenhagen, offering a sense of comfort and contentment.

For an authentic hygge experience, visit during the colder months when the city's charm shines brightest. Twinkling lights line the streets, and indoor spaces become sanctuaries of coziness. Whether gathering with friends over a meal or enjoying a quiet moment by the water, Copenhagen invites you to slow down and savor the simple pleasures of life.

CAFES

My favourite Cafes in Copenhagen

Apotek 57

Apotek 57 is an eatery nestled within the stylish Frama Studio, offering a menu that changes with the seasons for breakfast and lunch. The restaurant is known for its classic Copenhagen set, creamy porridge, and signature breakfast dishes like savory waffles, along with house-made sweet pastries. Sharing space with the Frama design showroom, Apotek 57 provides an appealing visual experience, allowing guests to appreciate cool design pieces while enjoying their meal.

Address:

Fredericiagade 57

What people like about it

A place to satisfy your palate and eyes.Beautiful breakfast / light lunch spot in Copenhagen center. Choose from a classic Copenhagen set (home baked sourdough buns with whipped butter and cheese), perfectly creamy porridge or signature breakfast dishes such as savoury waffle with egg salad or with whipped mascarpone, elderflower and rhubarb jam.

Collective Bakery

Collective Bakery is known for its delectable pastries, gourmet coffee, and ice cream. Their espresso soft serve is a must-try during summer, while their coffee and pastries are popular all year round. The bakery is also credited for introducing the light roast style coffee to its locale, a style that has since gained widespread popularity.

Address:

Nørrebrogade 176

What people like about it

Delicious pastries, coffee and icecreamThere are many reasons to stop by either one of Coffee Collectives' many cafes or their bakery. In the summertime, their espresso softserve is a must and all year round so are their coffee and pastries. Everyone in Copenhagen owes them a big thanks as they were the ones that introduced good coffee to the city with the light roast style that has since become the city’s style almost everywhere.

Learn more

Lille Bakery

Lille Bakery is a neighborhood spot known for its exceptional sourdough bread, crafted using Danish Organic Flour from local farmers. The bakery transforms into a cozy bistro on Friday and Saturday nights, adding a unique twist to its regular operations. Visitors particularly enjoy their sausage rolls and freshly brewed chai tea, making it an ideal place to sit back and watch the world go by.

Address:

Refshalevej 213A

What people like about it

Great and busy bakery and cafe in hipster Refshaleøen
